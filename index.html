About Us

What began with one friend officiating another friend's wedding grew naturally into Veggie Patch Ceremonies. Today, two ordained officiants and a small support team help couples create warm, meaningful ceremonies throughout Western North Carolina.

We tailor every ceremony to the couple in front of us. Whether your day is traditional, spiritual, secular, quirky, intimate, or completely unexpected, we want it to sound and feel like you.
